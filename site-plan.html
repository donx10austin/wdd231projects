RheignCred Orthopedic Hospital

Site Planning Document

1. Site Name

Name: RheignCred Orthopedic Hospital

Reasoning: The name combines "Rheign" (leadership/reign over bone health) with "Cred" (Credibility). This establishes professional trust for patients in Lagos.

2. Site Purpose

To provide a professional digital gateway for patients to access specialized orthopedic information, book consultations, and verify the expertise of medical specialists.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Injured Athlete

"I am an athlete who just injured my knee during a match. Can I book an appointment with a sports medicine specialist today?"

Scenario 2: The Concerned Caregiver

"My grandparent needs a hip replacement. Does RheignCred have the credentials and a clear location in Lagos I can drive to?"

4. Color Scheme

Primary: Navy (#0b2c5a) - Authority and trust.

Secondary: Teal (#17a2b8) - Medical accents.

Accent: Coral (#ff6b35) - High-priority CTAs.

5. Typography

Primary Font: Roboto

Usage: Universal. Weights: 900, 400, 300.
