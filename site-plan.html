1. Site Name

Name: RheignCred Orthopedic Hospital

Reasoning: Combines "Rheign" (authority over bone health) with "Cred" (Credibility) to build patient trust in the Lagos medical market.

2. Site Purpose

To provide a professional digital gateway for patients to access specialized orthopedic information, book consultations, and verify medical expertise.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Injured Athlete

"I injured my knee during a match. Can I book a specialist appointment today?"

Scenario 2: The Concerned Caregiver

"My grandparent needs a hip replacement. Is this hospital credible and where is it located?"
