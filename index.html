RheignCred Orthopedic Hospital

Site Planning Document

1. Site Name

Name: RheignCred Orthopedic Hospital

Reasoning: The name combines "Rheign" (suggesting sovereignty, leadership, and a "reign" over bone/joint health) with "Cred" (signifying Credibility and Credentials). This establishes immediate professional trust for patients in Lagos seeking specialized surgical care.

2. Site Purpose

To provide a professional digital gateway for patients to access specialized orthopedic information, book consultations, and verify the expertise of medical specialists, thereby streamlining the path to recovery and mobility.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1: The Injured Athlete

"I am an athlete who just injured my knee during a match. Can I book an appointment with a sports medicine specialist today?"

Scenario 2: The Concerned Caregiver

"My grandparent needs a hip replacement. Does RheignCred have the credentials and a clear location in Lagos I can drive to?"

4. Color Scheme

Primary: Navy (#0b2c5a) - Used for headers and primary text to show authority and trust.

Secondary: Teal (#17a2b8) - Used for sub-headings, icons, and medical accents.

Accent: Coral (#ff6b35) - Used for high-priority CTA buttons like "Book Now" and "Emergency."

5. Typography

Primary Font: Roboto (Variable)

Usage: Universal (Headings, Body text, and UI elements).

Weights: 900 (Headings), 400 (Body), 300 (Footer).
